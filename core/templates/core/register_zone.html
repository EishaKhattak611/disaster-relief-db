How to Get Coordinates

Step 1: Open Google Maps in a new tab

Step 2: Search for the disaster location by typing the city or area name (e.g. "Chitral", "Awaran Balochistan", "Swat Valley")

Step 3: Right-click on the exact location on the map

Step 4: The first option shows coordinates like 35.851800, 71.786400 — click it to copy

Step 5: The first number is Latitude, the second is Longitude. Paste them into the fields on the left.

Pakistan coordinate ranges:
Latitude: 24° (Karachi) to 37° (Chitral)
Longitude: 60° (Gwadar) to 77° (Azad Kashmir)
